What is Founders of Tomorrow?

FoT is Shark Tank for high schoolers. Students pitch real business ideas on a national stage, get mentored by industry experts, and compete for up to ₹10 lakhs in prizes. Created by IVB, India's first university built around entrepreneurship. It's completely free to enter.

How FoT Works

1
STAGE 1

School Level

ELIGIBILITY

Class XI, XII, completed Class XII, and drop year students, in teams of 1 to 4. Entry is free.

BUSINESS STAGE

Can be at any stage, from ideation to revenue generating.

SUBMISSION

Register your team and send in your business idea as a Word document, PowerPoint presentation, or video.

QUALIFICATION

Top 10 teams from each region qualify to Regionals.

🏆 TAKEAWAY
  • ₹5,000 Prize per qualifying team
  • 1-on-1 career guidance from industry experts
  • Recognition by IVB as Top 100 Upcoming Business Leader
2
STAGE 2

Regional Level

CITIES

Chennai, Bangalore, Hyderabad, Mumbai, Delhi, Kolkata. Teams register to their closest region.

ELIGIBLE PARTICIPANTS

Top 10 teams from each region from Round 1.

PRESENTATION

Online pitch to expert jury — 8-minute pitch + 5-minute Q&A.

QUALIFICATION

Top 2 teams from each region qualify for Nationals.

🏆 TAKEAWAY
  • ₹20,000 Prize per qualifying team
  • Direct admission offer to IVB's undergraduate program
  • Additional funding if joining IVB
  • Recognition as Top 25 Upcoming Business Leader
3
STAGE 3

National Finals

PARTICIPANTS

Top 2 teams from each of the 6 regions (12 teams total)

LOCATION

IVB Campus, Chennai (Express Avenue Chambers)

MENTORSHIP

Teams receive mentorship from IVB experts to fine-tune their idea and pitch before the finale.

FINAL PITCH

Grand finale pitch to a panel of celebrity judges including renowned entrepreneurs.
